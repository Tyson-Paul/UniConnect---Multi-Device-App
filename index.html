About UniConnect

A student portal designed for parents and teachers.

Why this portal exists

UniConnect was built to reduce the friction of locating student information. In many situations, the problem isn’t the lack of data—it’s how hard it is to find it quickly.

The portal focuses on clear records, structured profiles, and simple navigation, so parents and teachers can access the essentials without scrolling through clutter.

What it provides

The Students page presents a set of example profiles with consistent formatting. The Programs page lists university-level programs and summaries.

How access works

The landing page is public. Once a user logs in, UniConnect switches to the Portal view. This demonstrates protected navigation and a clear separation between public and private pages.

Design choices

The interface uses a white background, soft borders, and minimal accent color. The goal is to look professional and keep content readable.

Future improvements

A next step would be loading student records from a database instead of static cards, and adding role-based permissions for teachers versus parents.
